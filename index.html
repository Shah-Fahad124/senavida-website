Consumer Application

Personalized Wellness, Guided by AI

The Senavida Consumer App is designed to give people everywhere access to meaningful wellness guidance.Whether through traditional therapies or non-traditional practices, seekers can explore, discover, and connect with the right support through our thoughtful AI-powered companion, Zoe.

Zoe: Your Guide & Companion

Smart Discovery

An interactive chatbot that helps clients find practitioners and therapies aligned with their language, culture, gender, ethnicity, spiritual affinity, and healing goals.

Daily Guidance

Proactive check-ins that encourage clients toward the behaviors and protocols set in partnership with their therapist or coach.

360° Health Coaching

Integration of mind, body, and spirit with data from labs, wearables, and in-person assessments, all organized into a clear and actionable framework.

Human Connection

Zoe can even leverage the voice of a client's therapist or coach, making the experience more personal, warm, and humanistic.

The consumer app is where the Senavida vision comes full circle—bringing access, personalization, and guidance into the hands of every seeker, wherever they are on their healing journey.
